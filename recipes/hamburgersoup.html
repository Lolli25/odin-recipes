Spicy Hamburger Soup

Spicy hamburger soup is a hearty, rich, and flavorful soup made with ground beef, potatoes, and veggies in a spicy tomato beef broth.

This soup is an easy one-pot meal that is great for emptying the produce bin. The best thing about soup is that you don't have to follow the recipe exactly. You can add whatever vegetables you like and leave out any vegetables that you don't like. Personally, I add carrots, potatoes and onions because that's what my family will eat!
